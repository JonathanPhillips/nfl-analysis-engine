Understanding Advanced Metrics
Expected Points Added (EPA)

EPA measures the value of each play by comparing the expected points before and after the play. Positive EPA indicates the play improved the team's position.

Win Probability Added (WPA)

WPA tracks how much a play changes the probability of winning the game. More impactful in close games and late situations.

Success Rate

Percentage of plays that achieve their "successful" outcome (gaining enough yards considering down and distance).

Explosive Plays

Plays with significant yardage gains (20+ yards) or touchdowns that can change the momentum of a game.
